Google has made many headlines in past few weeks from open sourcing Machine Learning library TensorFlow to Machine Learning 101 event. Race to build ultimate AI is getting intense everyday. Microsoft has also jumped on this with Project Oxford and DMTK.


How would you program autonomous vehicle when only choices left are either kill driver or kill pedestrians? Do you see the moral and ethical dilemma here? Check out this article pointing out to to recent research paper on this topic and story on MIT technology review.
